ONLINE FEE DEPOSIT
 IMPORTANT NOTE:
  1. EXISTING STUDENT CAN DEPOSIT THEIR EXAM FEE ONLINE USING THEIR COMPLETE ROLL NUMBER (i.e. B/10/021 etc. ) AND PASSWORD AGAIN THE SAME ROLL NUMBER.
  2. EXISTING STUDENT CAN DEPOSIT THEIR SEMESTER/ YEAR FEE ONLINE. THEY HAVE TO ENTER THEIR COMPLETE ROLL NUMBER (i.e. B/10/021 etc. ) AND PASSWORD (AS ENROLLMENT NUMBER which is given in your previous semester/ year original receipt issued by SMS Varanasi Fee Counter)
  3. NEW ADMISSION CAN DEPOSIT THEIR FEE ONLINE BY ENTERING FORM NUMBER ISSUED BY SMS VARANASI/GIVEN INTO THE ALREADY FILLED -UP FORM.
  4. WHEN FEE IS DEPOSITED THROUGH ONLINE MODE, GET THE PRINT OF PROVISIONAL FEE RECEIPT FROM THE WEBSITE AND SHOW IT TO SMS VARANASI FEE COUNTER ONLY THEN ORIGINAL FEE RECEIPT WILL BE ISSUED TO THE STUDENT.
